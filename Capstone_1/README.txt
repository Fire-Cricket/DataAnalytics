# Sales Territory Analysis

## About
This project looks at sales performance for the territory managed by See Ellefson using the Sample_Sales database.

The goal was to understand how sales are changing over time, compare store performance, and make a recommendation for next quarter.

---

## What I Did
- Used SQL to analyze sales data  
- Found total revenue and date range  
- Looked at monthly revenue trends  
- Compared store performance  
- Built a dashboard in Excel  

---

## Key Findings
- Sales increased from 2022 to 2025  
- There is a big jump in revenue in 2025  
- New York and Rochester are the top-performing stores  

---

## Recommendation
Focus on the top-performing stores like New York and Rochester to increase revenue.  
Also improve lower-performing stores with promotions.

---

## Files in this Repo
- SQL queries  
- Excel dashboard  
- PowerPoint presentation  

---

## Conclusion
Sales are growing, and a few stores are driving most of the revenue.  
Focusing on those stores while improving others can help increase overall sales.
